Open Innovation Challenge

Smart Mattress 2.0

AI & Smart Manufacturing for Better Sleep

About the Company

Company Name: Aaka’s Innovation

Aaka’s Innovation is a fictional manufacturing company focused on developing next-generation consumer products using artificial intelligence (AI) and smart manufacturing technologies. The company aims to transform traditional household products into intelligent, data-driven solutions that improve user comfort, health, and sustainability.

One of Aaka’s Innovation’s core products is Mattress 2.0, an AI-enabled mattress designed to optimize sleep quality through intelligent design, material selection, and advanced manufacturing processes.

Seeker Description

This Open Innovation Challenge is launched by the Innovation and Advanced Manufacturing Team at Aaka’s Innovation. The team integrates AI, simulation tools, and smart manufacturing systems into product development.

Due to the complexity of combining sleep science, AI modeling, materials engineering, and scalable manufacturing, Aaka’s Innovation is seeking external ideas and technologies from innovators worldwide.

Theory: Open Innovation by Crowdsourcing

Open innovation is an approach where companies go beyond internal knowledge and actively involve external actors such as startups, researchers, students, suppliers, and users.

Crowdsourcing allows a company to open a problem to a broad audience, collect diverse ideas, and identify innovative solutions that may not emerge internally.

  • Access multidisciplinary expertise
  • Reduce development time and cost
  • Encourage creative and unconventional solutions
  • Accelerate innovation in complex systems

The Challenge

Background

Traditional mattresses rely on fixed structures and limited personalization. Sleep quality depends on body posture, weight distribution, materials, and comfort preferences.

Mattress 2.0 uses AI-based simulation and smart manufacturing to improve comfort, adaptability, and sustainability.

Technical Requirements

  • AI or simulation models for sleep comfort and pressure distribution
  • Smart material combinations or modular mattress layers
  • Data-driven customization methods
  • Flexible or mass-customized manufacturing concepts

Economical Requirements

  • Cost-effective large-scale manufacturing
  • Avoid overly complex or expensive components
  • Consider logistics, production, and maintenance costs
  • Ensure commercial viability

SDGs Impact

  • SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being
  • SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
  • SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Award

  • €3,000 financial reward
  • Collaboration opportunity on a pilot project
  • Recognition within Aaka’s Innovation network

Out of Topic

  • Unrelated to mattresses or sleep systems
  • Purely marketing concepts
  • No technical or manufacturing content
  • Not aligned with sustainability principles
