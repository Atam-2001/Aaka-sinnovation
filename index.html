Smart Mattress 2.0

AI & Smart Manufacturing for Better Sleep

About

Company Name: Aaka’s Innovation

Aaka’s Innovation is a fictional manufacturing company focused on developing next-generation consumer products using artificial intelligence (AI) and smart manufacturing technologies. The company aims to transform traditional household products into intelligent, data-driven solutions that improve user comfort, health, and sustainability.

One of Aaka’s Innovation’s core products is Mattress 2.0, an AI-enabled mattress designed to optimize sleep quality through intelligent design, material selection, and advanced manufacturing processes.

Seeker

The Open Innovation Challenge is launched by the Innovation and Advanced Manufacturing Team at Aaka’s Innovation.

The team integrates AI, simulation tools, and smart manufacturing systems into product development.

Due to the complexity of combining sleep science, AI modeling, materials engineering, and scalable manufacturing, Aaka’s Innovation is seeking external ideas, technologies, and concepts from innovators worldwide through an open innovation approach.

Theory: Open Innovation by Crowdsourcing

Open innovation is an innovation approach where companies go beyond internal knowledge and actively involve external actors such as startups, researchers, students, suppliers, and users.

Crowdsourcing allows organizations to open a problem to a broad audience, collect diverse ideas, and identify innovative solutions that may not emerge internally.

  • Access multidisciplinary expertise
  • Reduce development time and cost
  • Encourage creative and unconventional solutions
  • Accelerate innovation in complex systems

Aaka’s Innovation adopts this approach to address the challenges of designing and manufacturing an intelligent mattress that meets technical, economic, and sustainability goals.

The Challenge

Background

Traditional mattresses rely on fixed structures and limited personalization. Sleep quality depends on posture, weight distribution, materials, and individual comfort preferences.

Mattress 2.0 applies AI-based simulation and smart manufacturing to improve comfort, adaptability, and sustainability.

  • Adapt to different users
  • Manufactured efficiently at scale
  • Remain affordable and sustainable

Technical Requirements

  • AI or simulation models for sleep comfort and pressure distribution
  • Smart material combinations or modular mattress layers
  • Data-driven customization methods
  • Flexible or mass-customized manufacturing concepts

Economical Requirements

  • Cost-effective large-scale manufacturing
  • Limited complexity and component cost
  • Consider logistics, production, and maintenance
  • Commercial viability

SDGs Impact

  • SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being
  • SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
  • SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Award

  • €3,000 financial reward
  • Collaboration on a pilot project
  • Recognition within the innovation network

Out of Topic

  • Unrelated to mattresses or sleep systems
  • Pure marketing concepts
  • No technical or manufacturing contribution
  • Not aligned with sustainability principles
