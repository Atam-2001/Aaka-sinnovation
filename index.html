About the Company

Company Name: Aaka’s Innovation

Aaka’s Innovation is a fictional manufacturing company focused on developing next-generation consumer products using artificial intelligence (AI) and smart manufacturing technologies. The company aims to transform traditional household products into intelligent, data-driven solutions that improve user comfort, health, and sustainability.

One of Aaka’s Innovation’s core products is Mattress 2.0, an AI-enabled mattress designed to optimize sleep quality through intelligent design, material selection, and advanced manufacturing processes.

Seeker Description

This Open Innovation Challenge is launched by the Innovation and Advanced Manufacturing Team at Aaka’s Innovation.

Due to the complexity of combining sleep science, AI modeling, materials engineering, and scalable manufacturing, Aaka’s Innovation is seeking external ideas and technologies from innovators worldwide.

Theory: Open Innovation by Crowdsourcing

Open innovation is an approach where companies go beyond internal knowledge and actively involve external actors such as startups, researchers, students, suppliers, and users.

  • Access multidisciplinary expertise
  • Reduce development time and cost
  • Encourage creative and unconventional solutions
  • Accelerate innovation in complex systems

The Challenge

Background

Traditional mattresses rely on fixed structures and limited personalization.

Technical Requirements

  • AI or simulation models
  • Smart material combinations
  • Data-driven customization
  • Flexible manufacturing concepts
